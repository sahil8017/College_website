Campus Facilities

Wi-Fi Campus Auditorium Seminar Hall Medical Room Cafeteria Bank & ATM Parking Area Staff Room QR Coded Plants CCTV Security Public Announcement System

Wi-Fi Enabled Campus

The entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled, providing high-speed internet access to students and faculty. This connectivity supports e-learning, research, and seamless access to online academic resources from anywhere on campus.

Auditorium

Our state-of-the-art auditorium is equipped with modern audio-visual technology and has a large seating capacity. It serves as the venue for major college events, including national seminars, conferences, cultural festivals, and guest lectures.

Seminar Hall

The college has a well-furnished seminar hall, ideal for departmental workshops, presentations, and smaller academic gatherings. It is equipped with a projector and sound system to facilitate interactive sessions.

Medical Room

The health and well-being of our students and staff are a priority. The campus has a dedicated medical room with first-aid facilities and a qualified professional to attend to any immediate medical needs.

Cafeteria

The college cafeteria offers a variety of hygienic and delicious food options at affordable prices. It's a popular spot for students to relax, socialize, and grab a bite between classes.

Bank & ATM

For the convenience of students and staff, a nationalized bank branch and an ATM are available within the campus premises, facilitating easy financial transactions.

Parking Area

The college provides a spacious and secure parking area for students, staff, and visitors. The facility is well-managed to ensure the safety of vehicles.

Staff Room

Comfortable and well-furnished staff rooms are provided for faculty members, offering a quiet space for academic preparation, discussions, and relaxation.

QR Coded Plants & Trees

In a unique initiative to blend technology with nature, many plants and trees on our campus are tagged with QR codes. Students can scan these codes with their smartphones to instantly access information about each species, promoting interactive and environmental learning.

CCTV Enabled Campus

To ensure the safety and security of our students and staff, the entire campus is under CCTV surveillance. This system helps maintain discipline and provides a secure environment for all.

Public Announcement System

A centralized Public Announcement (PA) system is installed across the campus. It is used to broadcast important notices, announcements, and emergency information to all students and staff efficiently.
