Classrooms

Our college provides spacious, well-ventilated classrooms that create a conducive atmosphere for learning. Each room is equipped with comfortable seating and essential teaching aids to facilitate effective instruction and student engagement.

Smart Classrooms

To keep pace with modern educational technology, we have equipped several classrooms with smart boards, projectors, and audio-visual systems. These smart classrooms enhance the teaching-learning experience through interactive and multimedia-rich content.

Laboratories

Our college boasts well-equipped laboratories for various science and technical subjects, including Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology, and Computer Science. These labs provide students with hands-on experience and practical knowledge essential for their fields.

Dark Room

A specialized dark room is available for experiments in optics and photography. This facility allows students in Physics and related fields to conduct light-sensitive experiments in a controlled environment, crucial for understanding fundamental concepts.

Botanical Garden

Our lush Botanical Garden is home to a diverse collection of plant species, serving as a living laboratory for the Botany department. It provides an excellent resource for students to study plant morphology, taxonomy, and ecology in a natural setting.

Zoological Museum

The Zoological Museum houses an extensive collection of preserved specimens, skeletons, and models representing various animal phyla. It is an invaluable resource for students of Zoology, offering a visual and tangible aid to understanding animal diversity and evolution.
