Administration

Presently the college works under the able guidance of trusties:

On 12th September, 1898, three days after Sardar Dyal Singh Majithia’s death, his Will was opened, on the application of his nearest male relation Sardar Gajendra Singh Majithia, and in the presence of Lala Harkishen Lal and Mr. Joginder Chandra Bose, by Mr. B. H. Bird, Acting Registrar of the District. The will assigned some of the property to Sardar Gajinder Singh, made certain arrangements for the decent maintenance of Rani Bhagwan Kaur, the second wife of the Sardar, and created three Trusts with specific Committees of Trustees and specific properties for the creation and maintenance of a first class Arts College and a Public Library and for the maintenance of the Tribune Press and Newspaper. The largest and the most valuable portion of the property was assigned for the College, the establishment of which on new lines of instruction had always been the dream of the Sardar.

The clauses concerning the creation of the College ran as follows:

  • (a) All proprietary rights including accessory and other rights and lands, gardens and houses in the following villages in the District of Amritsar, viz:
    • Dyalpore
    • Burg (Nawabad)
    • Ruk Bhangwan
    • Putli
    • Bhalapind
  • (b) All my proprietary rights including accessory and other rights and lands, houses and gardens in the village of Mirzajan in the District of Gurdaspur.
  • (c) All my houses and lands in Lahore and its suburbs, excepting the house called the “Exchange” now occupied by Messrs E. Gillon and Co. Ltd.
  • (d) My house property in the city of Karachi, Sind.
  • (e) Any other land and houses that may hereafter be acquired by me in Lahore, Karachi or any other place.
  • (f) The eighty shares belonging to me and standing in my name in the Delhi Cotton Mills Company Ltd, and the forty shares belonging to me and standing in my name in the Krishna Mills Company Ltd., Delhi.
  • (g) All my shares in the Punjab National Bank, Lahore.
  • (h) All current and fixed deposits to my credit in the Alliance Bank of Simla, Lahore, in the Agra Bank, Lahore, in the Bank of Bengal at Lahore and Calcutta, and with Messrs Latham and Co. of Karachi.
  • (i) All sums of money due to me on promissory notes, bonds, hundis or cheques.
  • (j) Two gold necklaces set with pearls, rubies and diamonds now in the custody of my cashier Bhanga Singh at Amritsar.
  • (k) Furniture, books and book-shelves at my residential house at Lahore and books and book-shelves at my residential house at Amritsar.

That the said Committee of Trustees shall consist of the following members, viz.

  1. Babu Jogendra Chandra Bose, M.A., B. L., Pleader, Chief Court, Lahore.
  2. Babu Sasi Bhushan Mukerjee, M.A. BL Professor Govt. College, Lahore.
  3. Dewan Narendra Nath, M.A., District Magistrate, Montgomery.
  4. Mr. Charles Golak Nath, B.A., LL.B., Barrister-at-Law, Lahore.
  5. Mr. Harkrishen Lal, B.A., Barrister-at-Law, Lahore.
  6. Lala Ruchi Ram, M.A., Assistant Professor, Government College, Lahore.
  7. Mr. Golak Nath Chatterji, B.A., Assistant Professor, Govt. College. Lahore.
  8. Lala Sunder Das Suri, M.A. Asstt. Superintendent, Training College, Lahore.
  9. Babu Abinas Chandra Mazumdar of the North Western Railway Office, Lahore.

On 3rd May, 1910 Dyal Singh College, Lahore was opened. Dewan Anand Kumar re-established Dyal Singh College and Dyal Singh Library in Karnal, Haryana on September 09, 1949, which were founded on the excellent vision and will of Sardar Dyal Singh Majithia.

Dyal Singh College, Karnal enjoys the prestige of being one of the oldest higher education institutes in independent India. Continuing the legacy of Dyal Singh College, Lahore, established by the great academician, entrepreneur, visionary, and philanthropist, Sardar Dyal Singh Majithia, it was established in Karnal in 1949 by the great academic enthusiast, Dewan Anand Kumar Ji. Since its inception, the college has proceeded to attain high and higher destinations both in academic and co-curricular activities. Its dynamism, which is the result of the esteemed members of its managing committee, has enabled the college to keep up with the latest requirements in the academic sphere. Adapting itself to the evolving environment with increasing role of technology in education, the college has smoothly and easily encountered every new challenge.

The college provides its students the best of training in graduate and post-graduate courses in the streams of Science, Commerce, and Humanities. The dedicated teaching faculty, with a deep sense of commitment and sincerity, tries to develop various skills of students in their respective fields. The college, along with academic excellence, aims to instill in its students social, cultural and moral values so that they may contribute towards the betterment of the society, and the nation. We are proud that our students occupy top positions in different spheres of life and are men and women of consequence.

I assure all those who join this college that their dormant talents will find a fertile soil to bloom and they will become well-equipped, responsible human beings by the time they leave the portals of the college. My best wishes for all those who choose to take admission in this college.

D.K. Raina
President

Dyal Singh College, Karnal has been serving humanity since 1949 by imparting quality education in all the three streams namely Science, Commerce and Arts. Located in the heart of the city Karnal, the college boasts of a historically significant building which is a beautiful amalgamation of ancient and modern architecture. Ours is a NAAC accredited A+ grade, coeducational institute. The college is committed to a holistic development of students by providing them with a conducive environment. The highly qualified, dedicated faculty put in their earnest efforts to make teaching interesting, career-oriented, and thought-provoking. The amiable teacher-taught relationship creates a cordial ambience for the full blooming of their personalities. The progressive and encouraging attitude of the esteemed members of the management has resulted in the consistently rising bar both in academic and co-curricular activities of the college. The college has a caring environment with zero tolerance for indiscipline, ragging, gender-bias, and racism. The state-of-the-art infrastructure consists of well-equipped lecture rooms, laboratories, vast playgrounds, richly-laden library with an entire section dedicated to rare books on one hand and e-section on the other. The college organizes an array of round the year activities through various clubs and societies such as NSS, NCC, Cultural Committee, Sports Club, Women Development Cell, Eco-club etc. We go an extra mile to build MOUs with various institutes and industries to enhance the employability skills of our students. The chief emphasis is not only on nurturing the intellectual faculties of the students but also to turn them into responsible, disciplined and useful citizens with human values.

I accord a warm welcome to all those who are aspiring to join the Dyal Singh family.

With best wishes…

Ashima Gakhar
Principal

About College

Dyal Singh College, Karnal, as it stands today, is a premier co-educational centre of learning of Northern India. With a strength of 3693 students, the college has all the three streams of learning - Arts, Science and Commerce, with Post Graduate courses in English, Hindi, Political Science, Economics, Commerce and Chemistry, along with the add-on and vocational courses.
