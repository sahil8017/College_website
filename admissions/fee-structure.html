Important Notes on Fees & Concessions

  • Subject Combination, MDC, VAC, SEC, & AEC fees are included in the above fee structure.
  • Tuition Fee for female students in aided UG Courses is ZERO.
  • Tuition Fee for female students in aided PG Courses is ZERO if family income is less than ₹ 1.80 lakh per annum.
  • ZERO fee will be charged from SC students of Haryana if they are eligible for the PMS scheme.
