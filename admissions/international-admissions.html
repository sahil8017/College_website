Application Process

1

Visit the Portal

Click the button above to go to the "Study in India" portal, the one-stop platform for international student admissions in India.

2

Create Your Account

Register on the portal by providing your basic details to create a unique user account.

3

Complete a Single Application Form

Fill out one common application form to apply to multiple institutions, including Dyal Singh College, Karnal. Ensure you select our college during the selection process.

4

Upload Documents

Upload the required academic transcripts, letters of recommendation, and other necessary documents as specified on the portal.

5

Receive Admission Offers

If selected, you will receive admission offers from the institutions you applied to directly through the portal. Accept the offer from Dyal Singh College to confirm your seat.
