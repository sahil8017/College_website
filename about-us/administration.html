President's Message

Dyal Singh College, Karnal enjoys the prestige of being one of the oldest higher education institutes in independent India. The college, along with academic excellence, aims to instill in its students social, cultural and moral values so that they may contribute towards the betterment of the society, and the nation. I assure all those who join this college that their dormant talents will find a fertile soil to bloom. My best wishes for all.

Vice Admiral Satish Soni (Retd.)

Principal's Message

Dyal Singh College, Karnal has been serving humanity since 1949 by imparting quality education. The college is committed to a holistic development of students. The highly qualified, dedicated faculty put in their earnest efforts to make teaching interesting and thought-provoking. The chief emphasis is not only on nurturing the intellectual faculties of the students but also to turn them into responsible, disciplined and useful citizens. I accord a warm welcome to all aspiring to join the Dyal Singh family.
