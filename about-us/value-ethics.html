Core Values

The Dyal Singh College community is committed to a set of enduring Core Values that guide our Vision and Mission. These values are focused on the sustainable development of the institution and serve as a compass through changing times.

Inspiring In-Campus Environment

Taking inspiration from our founders, we preserve a serene cultural and aesthetic environment that is welcoming, secure, and well-suited for young scholars.

Value-Based Education

We value a student-centered teaching process focused on outcomes, discipline, punctuality, and social justice. We strive to inculcate good human and social values in our employees and students.

Transformational Learning

We promote multi-disciplinary research, creativity, innovation, and critical thinking. We value learning as a lifelong process of transformational growth.

Continuous Enrichment

We commit to fair, honest, and objective practices. Feedback is utilized for improvement, and we persistently interact with various organizations to expose faculty and students to the professional world.

Environmental Sustainability

We value and adopt practices to protect the environment for future generations and share these values with others.

Student Centric System

We are committed to fostering the professional and personal growth of all students. These interests are the primary focus of college decisions and activities.

Educational Competence

Our college focuses on making students good human beings and successful professionals through strategic teaching of both theoretical and practical aspects of education.

Human Values

Basic human values are at the core of being human and include truth, honesty, loyalty, love, and peace. They bring out the fundamental goodness of human beings and society at large.

Importance of Human Values:

  • Provide understanding of attitudes, motivation, and behaviors.
  • Influence our perception of the world around us.
  • Represent the interpretation of “right and wrong.”
  • Provide a way to understand humans and organizations.

The five core human values we uphold are: Right Conduct, Peace, Truth, Peaceful Co-existence, and Discipline. These values are an integral part of one's personality and affect the employability quotient. Dyal Singh College is committed to the core human values mentioned in the Human Rights Charter.

Professional Ethics

The college upholds a strict code of professional ethics for all its members to ensure a respectful, responsible, and cooperative environment.

For Teachers:

Teachers are expected to adhere to a responsible pattern of conduct, seek continuous professional growth, perform their duties with dedication, and deal justly and impartially with students. They should respect students' rights, encourage their development, and maintain positive relationships with colleagues, authorities, and guardians.

For Non-Teaching Staff:

Non-teaching staff must adhere to a responsible pattern of conduct, maintain up-to-date records, and operate with transparency and accountability. They are expected to cooperate with colleagues, assist students with administrative matters, and treat all members of the college community with respect and professionalism.
