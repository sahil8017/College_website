Our Commitment to Quality

Pursuing excellence in education and research for nation-building.

Quality Policy

We at Dyal Singh College, Karnal are committed to the pursuit of excellence in the field of education and research in order to lead to the development of a competent workforce and contribute to nation-building. We endeavor to achieve this by continually improving our quality management systems. For this purpose, our Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) works tirelessly for quality assurance and improvement.

Quality Objectives

  • To increase stakeholder satisfaction, annually measured by the Research and Innovation surveys.
  • To implement at least one major improvement in each core and support process.
  • To implement transformation initiatives each year.

Quality Initiatives & Measures

  • Conformity to the regulatory requirements of UGC, Kurukshetra University, and the Government of Haryana.
  • Well-defined codes of conduct for teaching, non-teaching staff, and students.
  • Maintaining state-of-the-art infrastructure and a congenial learning environment.
  • Up-gradation of faculty through staff development programmes, industry interaction, seminars, and educational tours.
  • Measuring stakeholder satisfaction periodically through feedback, placements, and achievements.
  • Adoption of innovative teaching methods and quality control mechanisms.
  • Inculcating moral and ethical values among students and staff.
  • Collaborating with industry and other institutions for holistic development.
  • Disseminating knowledge through continuing education and extension activities.
  • Promoting, transmitting, and preserving our culture and heritage.
