Our Official Logo & Motto

तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय

(Tamaso Mā Jyotirgamaya)

“From darkness, lead me to the light.”

This profound verse from the Brihadaranyaka Upanishad serves as our guiding principle. It encapsulates our mission to dispel the darkness of ignorance with the light of knowledge, wisdom, and truth, empowering our students to become enlightened citizens.
