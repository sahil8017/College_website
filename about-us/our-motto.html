Our Guiding Motto

A principle that defines our educational philosophy.

"Gather Ye the Wisdom of the East & the West"

This motto embodies the visionary spirit of our founder, Sardar Dyal Singh Majithia. It reflects our core belief in creating a harmonious blend of traditional and modern knowledge. We are dedicated to providing a liberal education that integrates the rich intellectual heritage of the East with the scientific and rational advancements of the West, preparing our students to be global citizens with deep-rooted values.
