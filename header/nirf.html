About the NIRF

The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) was established in September 2015 by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. It provides a methodology to rank higher education institutions across the country based on a set of objective parameters.

The parameters broadly cover “Teaching, Learning and Resources,” “Research and Professional Practices,” “Graduation Outcomes,” “Outreach and Inclusivity,” and “Perception.” This framework helps students and stakeholders make informed choices and encourages institutions to strive for excellence.
